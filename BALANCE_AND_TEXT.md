# PUNI Life Balance And Text

## Initial Values

- Hunger: 80
- Happiness: 80
- Cleanliness: 80
- Energy: 80
- Affection: 10
- Level: 1
- EXP: 0
- Coin: 100

## Care Actions

| Action | Effect | Hidden Growth |
|---|---|---|
| Feed | Hunger +25, Affection +3, Coin -10 | Kindness +1 |
| Play | Happiness +20, Energy -10, Affection +5, EXP +5 | Sensitivity +3, Kindness +2 |
| Clean | Cleanliness +30, Happiness +5, EXP +3 | Kindness +4 |
| Sleep | Energy +40, Hunger -5 | Sensitivity +1 |
| Study | Happiness -3, Energy -10, EXP +8 | Intelligence +5, Sensitivity +1 |
| Train | Energy -15, EXP +8 | Strength +5, Courage +3 |

## Unlocks

- Egg: Feed, Play
- Baby: Feed, Play, Clean, Sleep
- Young: all actions
- Evolved: Feed, Play, Clean, Sleep

## Evolution Routes

- Sunny: default positive route, especially high Happiness and Affection
- Scholar: Intelligence 40 or higher and dominant
- Brave: Strength 40 or higher, or Courage 40 or higher
- Forest: Kindness 35 or higher, Cleanliness 60 or higher, Kindness dominant
- Shadow: Neglect 50 or higher, or low Happiness with high Neglect

## UI Text Tone

- Short, gentle, and readable on mobile.
- Avoid long tutorial copy in the main screen.
- Use action feedback messages:
  - `PUNI enjoyed the snack`
  - `PUNI had fun`
  - `PUNI is clean`
  - `PUNI rested`
  - `PUNI studied`
  - `PUNI trained`
